Welcome to Sai Paithan

Beutifying since 15 Years

Paithani (Marathi: पैठणी) is a variety of sari, named after the Paithan town in Aurangabad Maharashtra state where they are woven by hand. Made from very fine silk, it is considered as one of the richest saris in India. It is one of the most famous saris in India. It is also considered the finest silk in India.
